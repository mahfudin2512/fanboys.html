FANBOYS: The Power of Coordination

Learn about the seven coordinating conjunctions in English using the acronym FANBOYS

Conjunction Meaning Example
For Reason/Cause I stayed home, for it was raining.
And Addition She plays the guitar and sings beautifully.
Nor Negative alternative He doesn't eat meat, nor does he drink milk.
But Contrast I wanted to go, but I was too tired.
Or Choice You can have tea or coffee.
Yet Unexpected contrast It was sunny, yet quite cold.
So Result He was late, so he missed the bus.
